Frequently Asked Questions

At Foto Studio Artiglio, I specialize in capturing weddings and other significant church occasions. I also offer portrait sessions, engagement shoots, and family photography to help you preserve all your cherished moments.

It's best to book my services as early as possible to ensure availability, especially during peak wedding season. Ideally, booking 6-12 months in advance is recommended.

My wedding photography package includes a pre-wedding consultation, full-day coverage on the wedding day, high-resolution edited photos, and an online gallery for easy sharing and downloading. Custom packages can be tailored to meet your specific needs and preferences.
